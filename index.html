Introduction

A college is an academic institution or a constituent part of one. A college may be a degree-awarding educational institution. So, college is the place where one can get higher education for achieving his or her career goal. College is indeed necessary for each and every one. Thus for a successful career, the student needs knowledge, confidence, academic and technical skills, career opportunities and social skills
